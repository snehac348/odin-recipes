Samosa

Description

Samosa is a dish originating from the Indian subcontinent. It is a popular dish of South Asia.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan and add onions and fry for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Add the masala and mix well.
  4. Add the cooked rice and mix well.
  5. Add the samosa and mix well.
